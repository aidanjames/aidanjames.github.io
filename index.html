Tour rules

The competition will conclude at 11:59 PM on January 10, 2023. Participants may submit as many rounds, played in the stableford format and following standard golf rules, as they wish before the competition end date. These rounds must be witnessed by another MGA tour player to be considered valid. The player's total score will be determined by the sum of their top 5 stableford point rounds. The individual with the highest total score will be declared the winner and will be treated to a dinner paid for by the two runners-up. There is no second place prize.
