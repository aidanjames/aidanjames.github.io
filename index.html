Tour rules

The competition end date is at 23:59 on 01/10/2023.

A player may submit as many "qualifying scores" as they like before the competition end date. A "qualifying score" is any round played within the competition period, stableford format, normal golf rules (no quirky mulligans etc.!). A round must be witnessed by at least one other MGA tour player to qualify.

The sum of a player's best 5 rounds (Stableford points) will comprise their total points score. The player with the highest total points score at the competition end date will be declared the winner.

The winner's prize will be a dinner paid for by the two losers. There is no such thing as a runner up. You are either a winner or a loser.
